College Professor

Luiz started his career life as a college professor, teaching classes on tourism and the skills needed to be a travel agent and start a travel agency. He taught for two years before heading to the high seas for his next career opportunity.

Assistant Shore Excusion Manager

After the two years of teaching, Luiz wanted to discover the world, so he got a job with Royal Caribbean Cruise Lines and started as an Assistant Shore Excursion Manager. In the position he sold the Royal Caribbean tours that the guests took at each port while getting to see the world for himself.

Next Cruise Sales

It wasn't long that into his tenure with Royal Caribbean Cruise Lines that he got promoted to a Loyalty and Cruise Sales Associate. In his new role he got to interact with the top sailors who cruised with Royal Caribbean the most and sold the guests their next cruise while they were enjoying their current cruise. Due to Luiz's hard work and focus, he moved into the Next Cruise Sales Manager role on board where he led a team to achieve their monthly sales goals.

Inventory Specialist

While working on board a Royal Caribbean Cruise Ship, Luiz met his husband Scott and it wasn't long before they chose to start the next chapter of their lives together. In the spring of 2017, Luiz and Scott moved to Orlando, FL where Luiz got a job at Walt Disney World. He started in the call center booking Disney vacations for future Walt Disney World guests. He moved quickly to the sports group team where he used his selling skills to book Disney vacations for different sports teams coming to compete at Disney tournaments throughout the year. After two years with sports group he moved to his current job of Inventory Specialist where he assigns guest rooms at the Disney Resort to maximize revenue.
